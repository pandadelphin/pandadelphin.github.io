Data Privacy Policy for VR Arena Mobile - Multiplayer Shooter

Effective date: 11/20/2023

  1. 1. Introduction

    This Data Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect personal information from users of VR Arena Mobile, our multiplayer shooter game available on Android platforms. This policy complies with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other relevant data protection laws applicable in the European Union (EU). By playing VR Arena Mobile, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

  2. 2. Data Collection

    2.1. Personal Information: When you play VR Arena Mobile, we may collect certain personal information, including your Google Play usernames, IP address, device information, and gameplay data.

    2.2. Google Play Usernames: We collect Google Play usernames to facilitate multiplayer interactions and to help identify you within VR Arena Mobile.

    2.3. IP Address and Device Information: We may collect your IP address and device information to ensure the security and functionality of VR Arena Mobile and to troubleshoot technical issues.

    2.4. Gameplay Data: We collect gameplay data, including in-game actions, achievements, and progress, to enhance the gaming experience and optimize game mechanics within VR Arena Mobile.

  3. 3. Data Use and Purpose

    3.1. Multiplayer Interactions: We use Google Play usernames to enable multiplayer interactions and to allow you to connect and play with other users within VR Arena Mobile.

    3.2. Game Optimization: We use gameplay data to improve and optimize the game mechanics, balance, and overall user experience within VR Arena Mobile.

    3.3. Support and Troubleshooting: IP addresses and device information are used for technical support, to address technical issues, and to prevent fraudulent activities related to VR Arena Mobile.

    3.4. Communications: We may use your contact information to send important updates, announcements, or respond to your inquiries related to VR Arena Mobile.

  4. 4. Data Sharing

    4.1. Third-Party Service Providers: We may share some of your personal information with third-party service providers who assist us in providing VR Arena Mobile's services, such as cloud hosting, analytics, or customer support.

    4.2. Legal Requirements: We may share your personal information if required by law or to protect our legal rights and interests related to VR Arena Mobile.

  5. 5. Data Security

    We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard your personal information and prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction within VR Arena.

  6. 6. Data Retention

    We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy and to comply with legal obligations related to VR Arena Mobile.

  7. 7. Your Rights

    7.1. Access and Rectification: You have the right to access and update your personal information used in VR Arena Mobile. If you wish to do so, please contact us using the provided contact details.

    7.2. Data Deletion: You can request the deletion of your personal information from VR Arena Mobile's systems. Please note that this may result in the loss of your game progress and achievements

  8. 8. Responsible Legal Entity

    Reference is made to the legal entity Marina Dullnig (leixit.com) identified in the Google Play Store listing or within the app as the responsible party. Users are encouraged to refer to the specific details provided in the app's description or Google Play Store listing for information regarding the responsible legal entity.

  9. 9. Changes to this Policy

    We may update this Data Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our data practices within VR Arena Mobile. Any updates will be posted on this page, and the effective date will be revised accordingly.

  10. 10. Contact Us

    If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Data Privacy Policy or the use of your personal information in VR Arena Mobile, please contact us at pandadelphin@gmail.com.
